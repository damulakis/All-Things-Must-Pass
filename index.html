All Things Must Pass

My favourite song from this album is: My Sweet Lord

Summary

All Things Must Pass was released by Apple Records in November 1970, just seven months after the group's breakup was confirmed. The album's lyrics reflect both Harrison's notorious dark side as well as the spirituality he'd found in his embrace of Hinduism.
